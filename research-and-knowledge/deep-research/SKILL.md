# Deep Research

This skill enables the agent to perform comprehensive research on a given topic. The agent can gather information from various sources, synthesize the findings, and provide a detailed report.

## Workflow

1.  **Define the research scope:** The agent will work with the user to define the scope of the research, including the key questions to be answered and the desired level of detail.
2.  **Gather information:** The agent will search for and collect information from a wide range of sources, including academic papers, industry reports, news articles, and websites.
3.  **Synthesize and analyze information:** The agent will analyze the collected information, identify key themes and patterns, and synthesize the findings into a coherent narrative.
4.  **Generate a research report:** The agent will produce a comprehensive report that summarizes the research findings, including citations and a list of sources.

## Usage

To use this skill, provide the agent with the research topic and any specific questions you want to be answered.

**Example:**

```
Research the impact of artificial intelligence on the future of work.

Key questions:
- What are the potential benefits and challenges of AI in the workplace?
- Which industries are most likely to be affected by AI?
- What skills will be in demand in the future of work?
```
