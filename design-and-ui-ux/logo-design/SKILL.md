## Workflow

1. **Gather Requirements**: Understand the brand identity, target audience, and desired style of the logo.
2. **Generate Concepts**: Brainstorm and sketch multiple logo concepts.
3. **Refine and Iterate**: Select the most promising concepts and refine them based on feedback.
4. **Finalize**: Create the final logo in various formats and sizes.

## Usage

To use this skill, provide a detailed brief of your logo requirements. The more information you provide, the better the outcome.

## Example

**User Request**: "I need a logo for my new coffee shop, 'The Daily Grind'. I want a minimalist design that incorporates a coffee bean."

**Agent Response**: The agent will then follow the workflow to generate logo concepts, refine them, and deliver the final logo files.
