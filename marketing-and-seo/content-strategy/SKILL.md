## Workflow

1.  **Define Goals and KPIs:** Clearly define the objectives of the content strategy (e.g., increase brand awareness, generate leads, improve customer retention) and establish key performance indicators (KPIs) to measure success.
2.  **Target Audience Research:** Conduct thorough research to understand the target audience's needs, pain points, and online behavior. Create detailed buyer personas to guide content creation.
3.  **Content Ideation and Planning:** Brainstorm content ideas that align with the target audience's interests and the defined goals. Develop a content calendar to plan and schedule content publication.
4.  **Content Creation and Optimization:** Create high-quality, engaging content in various formats (e.g., blog posts, articles, videos, infographics). Optimize content for search engines (SEO) to improve visibility.
5.  **Content Distribution and Promotion:** Promote content across multiple channels (e.g., social media, email marketing, paid advertising) to reach the target audience.
6.  **Performance Analysis and Iteration:** Regularly analyze content performance against the defined KPIs. Use the insights to iterate and improve the content strategy over time.

## Usage

This skill can be used to:

*   Develop a new content strategy from scratch.
*   Audit and improve an existing content strategy.
*   Generate content ideas and create a content calendar.
*   Optimize content for search engines.
*   Promote content and analyze its performance.

## Example

**User Request:** "I want to create a content strategy for my new SaaS product that targets small businesses."

**Agent's Action:**

1.  The agent uses the `content-strategy` skill to guide the user through the process of defining their goals, researching their target audience, and brainstorming content ideas.
2.  The agent helps the user create a content calendar and provides recommendations for content formats and distribution channels.
3.  The agent assists the user in creating and optimizing a few initial pieces of content.
4.  The agent shows the user how to track the performance of their content and make data-driven decisions to improve their strategy.
