## Keyword Research Skill

This skill performs in-depth keyword research to uncover valuable search terms for SEO and content strategy.

### Workflow

1.  **Seed Keyword Input**: The user provides a seed keyword or a general topic.
2.  **Keyword Expansion**: The skill uses various search techniques to generate a broad list of related keywords, including long-tail variations and questions.
3.  **Data Retrieval**: For each keyword, the skill gathers important metrics such as search volume, keyword difficulty, and cost-per-click (CPC) data.
4.  **Competitive Analysis**: It analyzes the top-ranking content for the most promising keywords to understand the competitive landscape.
5.  **Keyword Grouping**: Keywords are organized into thematic clusters based on user intent and semantic relevance.
6.  **Report Generation**: The final output is a detailed report that includes the keyword clusters, their associated metrics, and strategic recommendations.

### Usage

To use this skill, provide a seed keyword or topic. The skill will handle the entire research process and deliver a comprehensive report.

### Example

**User Request:**

"Perform keyword research for the topic 'artificial intelligence in marketing'."

**Expected Output:**

A detailed report in Markdown format, containing:

*   **Primary Keywords**: High-volume, relevant keywords.
*   **Secondary Keywords**: Supporting keywords and phrases.
*   **Long-Tail Keywords**: Niche, specific search queries.
*   **Question-Based Keywords**: Common questions users are asking.
*   **Keyword Metrics**: A table with search volume, difficulty, and CPC for each keyword.
*   **Competitive Insights**: A summary of the top-ranking content and identified content gaps.
*   **Strategic Recommendations**: Suggestions for content creation and SEO optimization based on the research.
