## Workflow

This skill is designed to generate high-quality marketing and sales copy. The workflow is as follows:

1. **Define the Goal:** The user provides the objective of the copy (e.g., increase conversions, drive traffic, generate leads).
2. **Identify the Target Audience:** The user describes the target audience for the copy.
3. **Gather Key Information:** The user provides essential details about the product, service, or offer.
4. **Generate Copy:** The skill generates a variety of copy options based on the provided information.
5. **Refine and Iterate:** The user can request revisions to the generated copy to better suit their needs.

## Usage

To use this skill, provide a clear and concise prompt that includes the following information:

- **Goal:** What is the primary objective of the copy?
- **Audience:** Who are you trying to reach?
- **Product/Service:** What are you promoting?
- **Key Selling Points:** What are the main benefits and features?
- **Call to Action:** What do you want the reader to do next?

## Example

**Prompt:**

"Write a Facebook ad for a new line of eco-friendly cleaning products. The goal is to drive traffic to our website. The target audience is environmentally conscious millennials. The key selling points are that the products are plant-based, non-toxic, and packaged in recycled materials. The call to action is to 'Shop Now' and visit our website."
